E-Summit’23 is here - get your suits on!

Dear future entrepreneurs,
Greetings from KIIT Entrepreneurship Cell! We hope you all are greatly enjoying the Karnival. Well, to add to it, the Cell is back with E-Summit’23. E-Summit is the yearly flagship affair of the KIIT Entrepreneurship Cell that aims to promote interaction among industry leaders and students. The event provides a platform for the students of KIIT University to exhibit their entrepreneurial flair through keynote speaker sessions and workshops. The KIIT E-Cell will host this year's Entrepreneurship Summit on the 11th and 12th of February. Here's the time to iron that tie and get your tux on, as we've got a situation! Now that you’re back at the University and enjoying our very own KiiT 25 years celebration of Karnival, get your suits on as the Cell has planned a loaded 2 days’ workshop with a few of the best entrepreneurs and strategists out there starting with Sandeep Jain, Arsh Goyal, Prasun Kumar, etc. . Best part, absolutely free of cost. Harvey said “You always have a choice.” Now up to you all, whether you choose to grab this amazing opportunity or jump on it.

Day 1(9:00am to 5:00pm): 11th February 2023 (MakeItBig, Negocio, Bourne Creative)

Day 2(9:00am to 4:00pm): 12th February 2023 (EMerge, Women on Web, Test the Waters of Bootstrapping)

Theme: Suits (the series)

Registration Cost: Free
